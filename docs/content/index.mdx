# Welcome to Pred

Pred is a prediction market where you can bet on real-world questions, and bet
bigger with leverage. Will a team win? Will a price be above a number on
Friday? You take a side, and if you are right, you get paid.

Pred runs on [Sui](https://sui.io). Under the hood it is built on DeepBook
Predict, Sui's on-chain prediction market layer.

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
**Pred is live on testnet, and the economy is still an experiment.**

You are trading with test funds, not real money. We are still tuning how the
pricing, lending, and pool math behave with real flow. Leverage on prediction
markets is genuinely hard to get right, so numbers and rules in these docs can
change as we learn. Treat everything here as a work in progress.
</Callout>

## Two things make Pred different

Most prediction markets just let you bet. Pred does two jobs at once.

1. **It is a bookmaker.** Pred quotes both sides of every market and pays
   winners out of losers' money. It never takes a side itself.
2. **It lets you use leverage.** You can borrow from Pred to make a bigger bet
   than your own cash would allow, up to 5x today.

The second part is the interesting one. Lending against a bet that can drop to
zero the instant a market resolves is a hard problem. Most of these docs are
about how Pred does that safely.
