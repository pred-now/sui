# Welcome to Pred

Pred is a prediction market where you bet on real-world questions, and you can
bet bigger with leverage. Will a team win? Will a price be above some number on
Friday? You pick a side. If you are right, you get paid.

Pred runs on [Sui](https://sui.io), built on top of DeepBook Predict, Sui's
on-chain prediction market layer.

> **Pred is live on testnet, and the economy is still an experiment.**
> You trade with test funds, not real money. We are still tuning how the pricing,
> lending, and pool math behave under real flow. Leverage on prediction markets
> is genuinely hard to get right, so any number or rule in these docs can change
> as we learn. Treat all of it as a work in progress.

## What makes Pred different

Most prediction markets only let you bet. Pred does two jobs at once.

1. **It is a bookmaker.** Pred quotes both sides of every market and pays winners
   out of losers' money. It never takes a side itself.
2. **It lets you use leverage.** You can borrow from Pred to make a bigger bet
   than your own cash would allow, up to 5x.

The lending half is the hard part. Lending against a bet that can drop to zero
the instant a market resolves is a real problem. Most of these docs are about how
Pred does that without going broke.
