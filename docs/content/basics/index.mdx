# The Basics

New to Pred, or to prediction markets in general? Start here. These four pages
explain the whole product in plain words, with no math.

- **[What is Pred](/basics/what-is-pred)** introduces the bookmaker idea and the
  spread.
- **[How Prediction Markets Work](/basics/prediction-markets)** explains prices,
  probabilities, and payouts.
- **[Trading on Pred](/basics/trading)** walks through signing in, depositing,
  betting, and withdrawing.
- **[Using Leverage](/basics/leverage)** covers betting bigger with borrowed money,
  and how Pred keeps that safe.

Once these make sense, move on to [How the Economy Works](/economy) for
the why behind it all.
