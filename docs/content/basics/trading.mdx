# Trading on Pred

This page walks through the path from signing in to getting paid.

## 1. Sign in

You log in with a method you already have. Pred supports Slush, Enoki, and
Privy sign-in. There is no seed phrase to write down to get started.

Behind the scenes, Pred creates a **proxy account** for you on Sui. This account
holds your funds and your positions while you trade. Pred manages the signing
for it so every action is a single, safe transaction. The full custody design
is in [Custody and Money Flow](/technical/custody).

<Callout type="info">
This is a custodial setup. It is what makes lending possible, because the
borrowed funds must not be under your direct control while a loan is open. You
can always withdraw your own equity to your registered address.
</Callout>

## 2. Deposit

Send USDC to your account to fund it. On testnet this is test USDC, not real
money. Your deposit is detected, swept into your proxy account, and credited to
your trading balance.

## 3. Place a bet

Open a market, pick YES or NO, and enter an amount. Pred shows you the price it
will fill at, including its spread. Confirm, and the position is yours.

You can close a position before the deadline by selling it back at the current
price, or hold it to resolution.

## 4. Get paid

When the market resolves, winning shares pay 1 dollar each and losing shares pay
0. Your balance updates automatically. If you ever stop interacting with a
resolved market, Pred can still redeem your winnings for you, so nothing gets
stuck.

## Your balance vs your equity

Two numbers matter, and they are not the same once you use leverage.

- **Balance** is the gross value sitting in your account.
- **Equity** is what is actually yours: your collateral minus any debt you owe
  minus fees.

You can withdraw up to your **equity**, never the gross balance. This is the
one rule that keeps borrowed money from walking out the door. Withdrawals are
sent only to the address you registered.

## Withdraw

Request a withdrawal of any amount up to your equity. Pred checks your equity
after settling anything pending, then sends the funds to your registered
address. Your keys are never shown, exported, or returned by any screen.
