# Trading on Pred

Here is the path from signing in to getting paid: sign in, deposit USDC, place a
bet, wait for the market to resolve, then withdraw your equity.

## 1. Sign in

You log in with a method you already have. Pred supports Slush, Enoki, and Privy
sign-in. There is no seed phrase to write down to get started.

Behind the scenes Pred creates a proxy account for you on Sui. This account holds
your funds and positions while you trade, and Pred manages its signing so every
action is a single safe transaction. The full custody design is in
[Custody and Money Flow](/technical/custody).

This is a custodial setup. It is what makes lending possible, because the
borrowed funds must not be under your direct control while a loan is open. You
can always withdraw your own equity to your registered address.

## 2. Deposit

Send USDC to your account to fund it. On testnet this is test USDC, not real
money. Your deposit is detected, swept into your proxy account, and credited to
your trading balance.

## 3. Place a bet

Open a market, pick YES or NO, and enter an amount. Pred shows you the price it
will fill at, including its spread. Confirm, and the position is yours. You can
close it before the deadline by selling back at the current price, or hold to
resolution.

## 4. Get paid

When the market resolves, winning shares pay 1 dollar each and losing shares pay
0. Your balance updates automatically. If you ever stop interacting with a
resolved market, Pred can still redeem your winnings for you, so nothing gets
stuck.

## Balance vs equity

Two numbers matter, and once you use leverage they are not the same.

```ts
balance = everything sitting in your account
equity  = collateral - debt - fees   // what is actually yours
```

You can withdraw up to your **equity**, never the gross balance. This is the one
rule that keeps borrowed money from walking out the door. Withdrawals are sent
only to the address you registered.

## Withdraw

Request a withdrawal of any amount up to your equity. Pred checks your equity
after settling anything pending, then sends the funds to your registered address.
Your keys are never shown, exported, or returned by any screen.
